About Us

Who are we?

Welcome to GeoQuester, the ultimate adventure app for explorers and adventurers alike! At GeoQuester, we believe that every corner of your city holds a story, a mystery, or a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to turn your everyday surroundings into an exciting playground of quests and challenges.

Where are we?

We are everywhere! Well, kind of... GeoQuester was born in the city of Perth, Australia, and currently, we are only operating quests in Perth. However, please do not fear, we are working hard to expand our quests globally! Our hope is that whether you're strolling through the streets of Perth, wandering the alleyways of Tokyo, or hiking the trails of Patagonia, you can embark on thrilling quests and discover the world around you in a whole new way.

How to play?

To begin, click on the "Play Now" menu option. This will lead you to a page where you can explore quests around your city. Next, select a quest of your choosing. Each quest involves 5 questions. These questions will require you to move around the quest's area, referring to various landmarks, structures and artefacts to answer the questions. You only progress to the next question if you answer correctly. Points are earned for accuracy and completion.
